Al Patro Logo

This project marked one of my initial forays into Adobe Illustrator. My aim was to create a minimalist design imbued with distinctive character. The meticulously handcrafted olive branch, a central element, was seamlessly integrated into both the logo and the comprehensive style guide developed for this restaurant branding project.
